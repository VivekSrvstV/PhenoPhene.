The database can be browsed and searched for all or individual type of phenotype present in each plant species and various information about the individual plant can be viewed. We have integrated a visualize and analyze tool in the database to identify phenotypic traits in a given parameters. We are in the process of integrating more phenotype data on datasets for additional plants.
